Graphic Design Fieldwork

The Graphic Design fieldwork program at Algonquin College is proud to partner with creative agencies in Canada's National Capital Region and around the world...

Fieldwork Highlights

  • The start date is March 10 to April 17, 2026.
  • Students work as designers for a minimum of 180 hours during the six-week period.
  • In-person mentorship is preferred, but remote work is accepted.
  • Students are expected to work similar hours as other employees.
  • Students anticipate mentorship as a member of a design team.
  • We expect our students to honour the confidentiality of your business.
  • Fieldwork can be salaried or not; that's up to you and the student to negotiate.

What we need from you

  • Perform a thorough interview of candidates.
  • Provide supervision and mentoring during fieldwork.
  • Provide a productive and safe work environment for our students.
  • Complete a short report on the student's performance during their fieldwork.

We recognize that hosting a student intern represents an added responsibility for your company...
Thank you!
